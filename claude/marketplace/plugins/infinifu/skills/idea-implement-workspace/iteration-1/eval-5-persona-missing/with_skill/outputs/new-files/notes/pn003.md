# Persona [[product]]

## name
Compliance Officer

## summary
Internal compliance role responsible for evidencing regulatory and
legal obligations to auditors. Touches the platform read-only to
extract audit artifacts (deletion logs, access logs, retention
attestations) on a periodic cadence — typically quarterly.

## primary_goals
- Produce auditor-ready evidence on demand without engineering escalation
- Trust the integrity of exported artifacts (tamper-evident, signed)
- Stay aligned with legal and retention policy in place

## open_questions
- Which regulatory framework(s) drive the quarterly cadence (SOX, GDPR, internal policy)?
- Are exports consumed only internally, or shared with external auditors directly?
- Should the role have its own UI surface, or are CLI / scheduled-job exports sufficient?
