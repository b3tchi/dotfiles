# Persona [[product]]

## name
Compliance Officer

## summary
Internal compliance owner who answers to legal and external auditors.
Periodically asks engineering for proof that data-retention policies
(e.g. adr0002 90-day reports retention) actually executed. Does not
have direct database access; consumes whatever artefact engineering
produces.

## primary_goals
- Produce defensible quarterly evidence of data deletions for legal
- Verify that what the system claims to have deleted, was actually deleted
- Detect tampering of the audit trail

## open_questions
- How is the compliance officer authenticated to retrieve the export
  (SSO is out of scope per adr0001 — basic-auth account or out-of-band
  hand-off)?
- Is one quarterly export enough, or do we also need on-demand
  exports for ad-hoc audits?
- Retention of the *export itself* — does the signed CSV need to live
  somewhere durable beyond legal's inbox?
