# Story [[platform-flow]] [[product]]

## role
[[pn002|platform-engineer]]

## want
verify that credential rotation succeeds and stays consistent across
all regions, not just the region the rotate call originates from

## because
[[sp001]] shipped rotation with a single-region synthetic check (in
fact, the synthetic check was deferred entirely — see [[adr0004]]).
During implementation it surfaced that cross-region rotation
correctness is unverified — replicas in other regions could lag, serve
stale credentials, or fail to flip alias versions, and we'd have no
signal until a service started 5xx-ing in production.

## acceptance_criteria
- Rotation triggered in region A is observable as live in all other
  regions within an explicit, documented bound (target: <60s)
- A synthetic check runs in every region during the rotation window and
  asserts zero 5xx in each region independently
- A rotation that fails to propagate to any region raises an alert
  without auto-rolling-back the successful regions
- Integration test simulates region lag and asserts the propagation
  bound is enforced
