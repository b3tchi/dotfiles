# Story [[knowledge-graph]] [[diagramming]] [[product]]

## role
[[pn001|developer]]

## want
see the relations between my AKM notes as an interactive graph

## because
the knowledge graph only exists implicitly in per-file wikilinks —
reading file-by-file hides clusters, link structure, and broken
references

## acceptance_criteria
- every note under docs/notes appears as a node; every wikilink
  between notes appears as an edge
- note type and lifecycle status are distinguishable at a glance
- dangling wikilinks (links to notes that do not exist) are visible
- the graph reflects note additions and edits without manual rebuild
- the same relation data is available in machine-readable form for
  scripts
