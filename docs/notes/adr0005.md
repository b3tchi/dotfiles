# ADR [[cat002]] [[product]]

## title
No d3d12 GPU acceleration outside the WSLg-blessed path on WSL2

## context
Mesa's d3d12 driver only presents correctly inside WSLg; under any
custom display stack its output is broken, and failures poison the
/dev/dxg channel for the whole VM until restart. Evidence in
[[poc001]] and [[poc002]].

## decision
GALLIUM_DRIVER=d3d12 is forbidden in non-WSLg display stacks (xrdp
sessions, custom compositors, nested X servers). Everything outside
WSLg renders on llvmpipe.

## consequences
Custom display stacks stay stable and the VM is protected from
GPU-channel poisoning, at the cost of no GPU acceleration over RDP.
Revisit if mesa's d3d12 present support matures upstream.
